Rendering GIS Server Tiles

georender

Symbolic map rendering service for Open Fantasy Maps.

Body

The symbolic renderer that gives the atlas its look. georender takes geospatial data, runs it through a JSON ruleset, and emits PNGs — as slippy-map tiles, as full bounding-box images, or as ad-hoc renders of arbitrary GeoJSON.

Where the cartographer’s red ink and the dotted park boundaries actually get drawn.
